# Sources & attribution

This skill is an **original synthesis**. The text was written for this pack — no passages, images, fonts, or
logos are copied from any source. The underlying material is widely-established design knowledge (visual
hierarchy, the 12 principles of animation, broadcast safe areas, accessibility standards), which consists of
facts and methods that are not themselves copyrightable. Brand and standard names (Disney, Netflix, BBC,
Google Cloud, SMPTE, WCAG, Material Design, etc.) appear only as nominative factual references — to name a
principle, standard, or example — not as endorsements or reproductions.

The CSS/Remotion snippets in `references/plate-textures.md` are short, generic implementation idioms
(backdrop-filter glass, SVG `feTurbulence` noise, gradient-border padding-box trick). They are common-knowledge
patterns, written from scratch here.

The following public resources were consulted as research while writing this pack. They are listed for credit
and as further reading; none were reproduced.
